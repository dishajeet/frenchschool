Meet the Founder

Expert Guidance Rooted in Canadian Bilingualism

Bonjour! I'm Catherine Dubois, the founder of École Canada French. With over 15 years of experience in bilingual education, I've worked as a certified TEF/TCF exam evaluator and a curriculum consultant for French immersion schools in Ontario and Quebec.

We started this school to address a common problem: generic language courses fail because they treat all students the same. Our online French tutor Canada programs separate learners into distinct paths, so that professionals get rigorous exam practice, adults get practical speaking habits, and kids get curriculum-aligned academic help.

Every tutor on our team is a native French speaker certified in Canadian teaching standards, trained to focus purely on your defined outcomes.

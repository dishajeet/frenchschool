Frequently Asked Questions

Conversational Classes FAQ

Understand how we group students, manage schedule changes, and help beginners achieve fluency.

I am an absolute beginner. Can I join?

+
Absolutely! We organize group circles strictly by skill level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced). If you are a beginner, your class will focus on basic structures, greetings, and simple sentences in a friendly atmosphere with other beginners, so you never feel overwhelmed.

What is the difference between group classes and private 1:1 lessons?

+
Group classes are highly social and focused on group interaction, structured conversations, and listening practice with peers. Private lessons are 100% tailored to you—the tutor matches your learning speed, targets your specific weak spots (e.g. grammar, work vocabulary), and lets you schedule lessons dynamically week-to-week.

What happens if I need to cancel or miss a private class?

+
We have a flexible 24-hour cancellation policy for private lessons. As long as you cancel or reschedule via your dashboard at least 24 hours before your session, you retain the class credit. Missed group classes cannot be rescheduled, but you will receive the class recap notes and audio file.

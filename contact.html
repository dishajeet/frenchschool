Cancellation & Rescheduling Policies

We work hard to protect our tutors' schedules. Review our core policies on private sessions and group circles.

What is the rescheduling policy for private 1:1 lessons?

+
To reschedule or cancel a private session, you must make changes via your student portal at least 24 hours in advance. If you submit a cancellation request within 24 hours of your class time or do not attend a scheduled session, the lesson credit will be charged in full.

What happens if we miss a group conversational class?

+
Group classes run on fixed schedules and cannot be rescheduled or credited. If you miss a class, you will receive our weekly review email containing the core audio pronunciation files, conversation themes, and homework worksheets so you don't fall behind.

How do subscription billings work?

+
All of our regular monthly tutoring packages operate on automatic recurring billing cycle. You can change your package frequency, upgrade, downgrade, or pause your subscription before the next billing date directly inside your account panel.

Answering Parent Concerns

Immersion Homework Support FAQ

Read how we help families who do not speak French at home succeed in French immersion systems.

My child is struggling, but I don't speak French. How can I help them?

+
This is the most common concern for French immersion parents! You do not need to speak French. Our program is designed to take the pressure off you. We handle all instruction, homework translation, and grammar explanation. Tutors provide you with a weekly progress report written in English, detailing what your child accomplished and how you can support their motivation at home.

How do you align with my child's school curriculum?

+
During your free trial lesson, we ask you to share your child's current school worksheets, textbooks, or notes from their school teacher. Our tutors match the vocabulary and grammar themes (e.g. past tense verbs, sentence structure) to ensure we are reinforcing what they are actively learning in class, rather than introducing competing materials.

Who are the tutors, and are they certified?

+
All of our tutors are native French speakers who are fully certified language teachers or have extensive background in Canadian K-12 education. They are specifically vetted for their patience, clear articulation, and experience working with young children online.

What happens if we have a busy school week and need to reschedule?

+
We understand that kids have sports and family schedules! As long as you reschedule via the student portal at least 24 hours before your session, you can select any other open time slot within that month at no additional cost.
